Welcome to Aberration!

Learn to survive on a malfunctioning ARK, where the internal atmosphere has leaked away, resulting in a harsh surface with intense radiation, and a plethora of lush biomes underground. With the maintenance systems of this ARK malfunctioning, the many hazards, creatures, and nature of the environment present a thrilling new world to explore and master.

The biggest ARK map ever!

With unparalled vertical space and an environment dangerous to flying creatures, you will learn new ways to traverse the dangerous world of Aberration.
